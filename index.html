Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A rich, flavorful Italian pasta dish made with minced beef, tomatoes, and herbs. Perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 minutes
  • Servings: 4 people
  • Difficulty: Easy

Dish Image

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 250g minced beef
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 400g tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until soft.
  2. Add garlic and cook for one minute.
  3. Stir in minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce, add oregano, then simmer for 25–30 minutes.
  5. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions.
  6. Serve spaghetti topped with the sauce and enjoy!

Tips

For extra flavor, add a splash of red wine while simmering the sauce.
